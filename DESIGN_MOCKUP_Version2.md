## Brand & Style

- Colors: Earthy greens, soft browns, white backgrounds
- Fonts: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Open Sans)
- Imagery: Indian farms, fresh vegetables, export shipping
- Icons: Organic certification, farm, airplane/export, cart
- Language: English (with option for Japanese, Hindi)
